Launching into CyberSecurity


The below objectives were accomplished

This module is designed to explain the increase dependency on proactive defense of Cyber Security and deeper understanding of modern information and system protection technology and methods.

  • Looked into the professional roles, test, and analyze systems to keep data and information safe from hackers and other external threats.
  • key role in securing information systems, by monitoring, detecting, investigating, analyzing, and responding to security.
  • Develop an understanding of the core concepts in Cyber Security such as cryptography, penetration testing and ethical hacking.
  • Engaged with programming concepts and practices necessary for facilitating security-based solutions to identified problems.
  • Core Cyber Security subjects like cryptography, ethical hacking and Ability to implement security information system.
  • Develop the ability to implement a secure information system using the principles and concepts of object-oriented approaches to solving a given problem, such as information system design and web development.
  • Discuss the ethics of professional development highlighting major ethical-social-corporate responsibilities underpinning the concepts of threats, securities and global impacts prevailing the recent technological innovations and future applications.
  • A deep understanding of Ethics — moral principles that governs cyber security

    • COLLABORATIVE LEARNING DISCUSSION 1

    Topic:

    Discuss why Cyber Security is now a global issue and why it is important for companies to invest in Cyber Security.

    MY INITIAL POST

    Cybersecurity is a global affair that requires international collaboration to safeguard cyberspace. The increase in Cyber threats is a significant risk to the global economy.


    Without Cybersecurity, the defence against data breaches, malware, Phishing, and other cyber-crimes; makes cyberspace desirable targets for cybercriminals. For example, the 2018 WannaCry ransomware attack, estimated 40% of worldwide healthcare institutions, paid an estimated £92 million due to a combination of ransoms and activities.


    Also, on 9 November 2021, The Spanish beer maker DAMM in Barcelona haltered production after a cyber-attack where the plant that produces a staggering 7 million hectolitres of beer a year was hacked. However, the famous Estrella Damm lager did not say if a ransom was required or paid to the cybercriminals.

    Therefore, the importance of cyber systems security cannot be overstated.

    Consequently, it is of the essence to protect sensitive personal data, governmental and industry information systems.


    To preserve the global trust of cyberspace and the avoidance of excessive breaches, the protection of digital identities is a swift necessity. Having a well-executed plan reviewed is the first step to keeping information safe.
